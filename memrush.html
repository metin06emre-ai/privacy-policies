Privacy Policy – MemRush

Last Updated: May 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how MemRush (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, shares, and protects user data in full compliance with Google Play Developer Program Policies and Data Safety requirements.

1. Data We Collect

To provide a functional and synchronized gaming experience, we collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected during account registration to secure your profile and enable progress syncing.
  • Username: A public identifier chosen by you for the leaderboard and profile display.

1.2 App Activity and Gameplay

  • Game Progress: Including levels reached, XP earned, scores, and daily mission completion status.
  • In-App Economy: History of reward claims and point exchanges.
  • Leaderboard Data: Your scores and ranking relative to other players.

1.3 Device and Identifiers

  • Unique Identifiers: Firebase User ID (UID) and advertising identifiers (Ad-ID).
  • Approximate Location: Country-level location derived from device settings to provide localized content.
  • Technical Logs: Device model, OS version, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your secure game account.
  • To synchronize your game progress across multiple devices.
  • To maintain the Global Leaderboard system.
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To analyze app performance and fix technical issues (bugs/crashes).

3. Third-Party Services

MemRush integrates trusted third-party SDKs provided by Google LLC:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, real-time database, and crash analytics.
  • Google AdMob: For delivering in-app advertisements.
  • Firebase Analytics: To understand user engagement and improve the game.

4. Data Retention

Your account information and game progress are stored as long as your account is active. We reserve the right to delete data from accounts that have been inactive for more than 24 months to protect user privacy.

5. Account and Data Deletion

We provide users with full control over their data. You can delete your information using either of the following methods:

Method A: In-App Deletion (Instant)
The most efficient way is to use the app. Go to Settings and tap DELETE ACCOUNT. This will immediately and permanently erase your profile, email, scores, and all progress from our servers.

Method B: Email Request
You may request deletion by emailing burkanemretokman@gmail.com from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 business days.

Note: Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

6. Security

All data is transmitted using secure HTTPS/TLS encryption. We utilize Firebase’s secure infrastructure to protect your information against unauthorized access.

7. Children’s Privacy

MemRush does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data without parental consent, please contact us immediately for data removal.
